## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Brand Personality
- **Confident, not arrogant** - We know profit leaks, but we're not condescending
- **Direct, not alarmist** - State facts clearly without fearmongering  
- **Smart, not complex** - Show expertise without drowning users in jargon
- **Helpful, not salesy** - Lead with value, not aggressive conversion tactics

### Writing Style
- Use specific numbers over vague claims ("$193K" not "lots of money")
- Lead with benefits, not features ("Find refund fraud" not "Advanced ML algorithms")
- Write like you're explaining to a CFO, not a data scientist
- Break up text with clear headlines, bullets, and white space

### Words to Use
✅ Profit, Revenue, Margin, Cash Flow, ROI, Bottom Line  
✅ Identify, Detect, Discover, Uncover, Pinpoint  
✅ Evidence, Impact, Action, Insight, Intelligence  
✅ Real-time, Instant, Automated, Continuous

### Words to Avoid  
❌ Revolutionary, Game-changing, Disruptive  
❌ Synergy, Leverage, Paradigm Shift  
❌ Next-gen, Cutting-edge (unless explaining actual tech)  
❌ Bleeding-edge, Best-in-class, World-class
